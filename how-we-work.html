How we work

We run fixed-scope assessments first. You get clarity, deliverables, and a plan you can execute. Implementation is optional and quoted after the assessment.

Principles

  • Fixed scope: defined deliverables, timeline, and fee.
  • Paid assessments first: no free diagnostics or informal reviews.
  • Clarity before build: we reduce risk by naming reality and sequencing work.
  • Implementation is separate: quoted once the plan is clear.
Boundary: If you want “a quick look,” that is an assessment.

What an engagement looks like

Step 1: Intake

We confirm the problem, constraints, and which service fits. No scoping marathons.

Step 2: Assessment

We inventory, map, validate, and identify risk and root causes. You get concrete deliverables.

Step 3: Recommendations

We provide a prioritized plan with sequencing and tradeoffs so leadership can decide.

Step 4: Optional implementation

If you want us to execute, we quote that work separately with its own scope and timeline.

Who this is for

  • Teams with real operational or technical risk and willingness to address it.
  • Organizations that want decisions and outcomes, not endless exploration.
  • Leaders who value clarity, documentation, and maintainability.

Who this is not for

  • Anyone seeking free troubleshooting or ad hoc advice.
  • “We want everything, but we have no budget” situations.
  • Open-ended engagements without defined deliverables.
